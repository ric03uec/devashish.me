Introduction to the blog
2020-09-16 14:04 schedule 30 s.

For the V4 version of this blog, I’ve decided to remove all the old entries and start from scratch. With one significant change though: posting tech content only.

Over time I’ve lost interest in writing travel blogs and inflicting my unfiltered thoughts on public forums. At the same time, I’d started documenting my experiences and learnings at work, conversations with other people in industry and takeaways from reading up on the topics that excite me.

I (re)started this blog with a few objectives

  • Document my journey in building tech solutions
  • Keep notes about interesting concepts, their implementations and other aspects
  • Have discussion(s) about the “right” way to solve problems

To new beginnings!
